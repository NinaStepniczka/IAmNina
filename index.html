What is good design?

Design is a foreign subject to me, but based on what little I do know (and with some help from the internet), I have compiled a few important elements. First, if the design is for a client, ensuring the design is communicative should be a high priority. It should get a clear message across without being too cluttered or too sparse. Therefore, it needs to be detailed and straightforward while also being visually pleasing. An element I would hope designers also value is originality. While some projects are likely more professional or plain, keeping designs unique help to make people want to look at it longer or thinking about it once they've looked away.
